New Year's Eve Makeup Guide

When it comes to New Year’s Eve makeup, what immediately springs to mind?

A sharp-winged eyeliner and a bold red lip makeup, perhaps? (Did we hit the nail on the head?) But wait, this isn't your typical 2023 vibe anymore, folks. As the countdown to the most glamorous night of the year commences, we're urging you to dial up the heat with glamorous eye makeup & lip makeup!

As simple as it may sound, we fully understand the ultimate struggle: lip colour and eye makeup combinations for that dinner date. I mean, smokey eyes or a popping eyeliner—decisions, decisions! And just when you master that, bam, you've got to sync up those lips. Anyone in panic mode? Fear not, we've got your back with the best Lipstick & Eye Makeup combinations to slay at this year-end party.

Pink Eyeshadow and Fuchsia Lips

Step into the spotlight with a dazzling pink monochromatic moment that's equal parts simplicity and striking allure! Dive into the magical realm of our 'Eye Lit You So!' Vivid Eyeliner - 01 Pink Sphinx, setting the stage for glamour. But we're not stopping there – ignite the scene with our 'Mettle Satin Lipstick' in the vivacious shade 'Elizabeth' for those premium-looking lips. This dynamic duo ensures your look is both modern and mesmerizing, proving that the allure of pink knows no bounds.

How to get the look

  1. Start with a pink eyeliner and trace your eyes with the desired look you want
  2. Top it up with the highly pigmented 'Contour De Force Eyes And Face Palette - 02 - Pink Pro.'
  3. Create a small wing with a black eyeliner or continue the drama with a pink liner
  4. For lips, line your lips with 'Lipping On The Edge Lip Liner - 04 Tan Fan' and fill in with a subtle nude pink lipstick

Matte Orange-Red Lipstick + Gold Eyeshadow

Elevate your makeup game with the sizzling fusion of an orange-toned red lipstick and shimmery gold eyeshadow, creating a look that's as fiery as it is captivating. For your eyes, dive into the opulence of the 'Two Good To Be True Dual Eyeshadow' in shade 03 Cinnamon & Nutmeg for a luxurious shimmer that catches the light with every blink. The rich pigments ensure a dazzling effect that complements the intensity of your lipstick. Pair your lids with 'Smudge Me Not Liquid Lipstick' in shade 10 Drop Dead Red that calls out Lady Danger.

How to get the look

  1. Create a siren eye effect with the help of our double-duty eyeshadow combo. Use the walnut brown shade to create a transition shade and top it off with a warm rose gold hue
  2. Take a black eyeshadow and create a long inner corner. Line the waterline too
  3. Start by colouring your pout with bold pigmented red lipstick and top it off with the 'Lip Zip Matte Topper.'
